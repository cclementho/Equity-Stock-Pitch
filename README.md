# Equity Stock Pitch – Dodge & Cox Competition (2024)

## Overview
This repository contains an equity research stock pitch on Dodge & Cox, prepared for a student investment competition. The project evaluates the firm’s business model, investment philosophy, financial performance, and long-term outlook.

## Objective
- Apply fundamental equity analysis in a competitive setting  
- Develop a clear, defensible investment thesis supported by qualitative and quantitative analysis  
- Communicate financial insights concisely through a professional presentation  

## Contents
- `Dodge & Cox 2024.1.pptx` – Stock pitch presentation, including:
  - Company overview and business model
  - Industry and competitive landscape
  - Financial performance and key metrics
  - Investment thesis, risks, and conclusion

## Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- Financial statement analysis
- Equity valuation concepts
- Investment research and critical thinking
- Professional presentation of financial insights

## Notes
This project was completed as part of a stock pitch competition and reflects my own analysis and conclusions.
